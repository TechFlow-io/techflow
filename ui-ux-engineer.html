UI/UX Engineer

Job Posted On:

May 20th, 2020

Essentials:

  • Min. 9 years of design experience.
  • Min 4 years of HTML, CSS and JavaScript Experience
  • Strong JavaScript.
  • Full-time devoted developer.
  • Very good English communication skills as it will require regular communication.

The individuals should have knowhow and expertise on any combination of the following:

ReactJS, NodeJS, ThreeJS, Laravel, MongoDB, SASS, PHP, MySql, WordPress, HTML, CSS, JS, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator

We are in search of people who have supreme aesthetic skills, who are looking to settle for nothing less than perfection, and who put their heart and soul into work. If you are competitive, creative, and have a zest to achieve something, then we are the company for you.

You, as an Engineer, should have the ability to work on tight deadlines, both independently, and with a team, and be an ambitious problem-solver.

Interested people may send their resume at careers@techflowllc.com
